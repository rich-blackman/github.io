Rogers Communications: E-commerce Software Development Life-Cycle

It All Began When...

Rogers Communications assembled a cross-functional team of Cable, Internet, and Wireless representatives to launch a combined E-commerce website for all its divisions.

The team worked with IT Development, Web Design, Payment Processing and 3PL Fulfillment vendors, plus Rogers' Networking and IT teams to implement the website.

I was hired within the Wireless Direct Sales group to define and implement web features, applications, and operational processes for the end-to-end Wireless sales experience.

Requirements, Design & Implementation

Worked closely with stakeholders across the Wireless organization to define the digital product roadmap, business rules and requirements, functional specifications, the sales funnels' user flows, and the Fulfillment and Customer Service operational processes.

Collaborated with my Cable and Internet counterparts and the Development DBA to define the user interface designs for a custom SQL Server based CRM application with Product Catalogue, Customer Service Order Management, and Reporting administration modules.

Created test requirements, plans and use cases; worked with vendors and Rogers ERP business owners to integrate their test environments with the website, and coordinated the end-to-end test cycles.

Maintenance & Enhancements

Business point of contact for stakeholders' product related change requests all major Wireless product launches and key strategic initiatives. I developed the online requirements and managed or coordinated the end-to-end site builds.

Partnered with stakeholders and IT to define and implement the sales digital roadmap projects based on the Wireless division’s strategies and product and network roadmaps, and the E-commerce team's capital projects.

Created business cases for the sales and operations related capital projects, including an automated anti-fraud application.

Created documentation on new or updated site features and processes for the E-commerce, Customer Service and other affected teams, and presented changes to the teams as needed.

Highlights Include:

Partnered with the Pricing team to create requirements for a dynamic price plan recommendation application within the sales funnel at site launch. Customers selected their monthly budget range plus anticipated calling and data usage and were displayed 3 recommended plans along with links to additional plans.

Partnered with the IT and the Product and Marketing teams to create requirements for the TeamSite dynamic CMS product webpage templates that interfaced with the product catalogue to display product comparisons and program-based pricing within multiple sales funnels: new customer, hardware upgrade, B2B corporate accounts, and bundled promotional offers.

Collaborated with the Wireless Intranet team to integrate the website's product CMS templates into the Rogers Retailer Portal allowing store employees to sell and activate customers' products in-store in real-time.

Created data maps for all product catalogue integration projects, including integration with the Billing and Inventory systems via API’s for Self-Service funnels.

Collaborated with Finance and IT to implement a negative-file (SQL table) of fraudulent purchases from all Wireless channels to automate anti-fraud processes. Any online order whose payment details were in the negative file was automatically cancelled before payment processing.
